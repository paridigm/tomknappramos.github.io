Fractal Maker

Highly intricate fractals can be generated by applying simple iterative equations with complex numbers. Such fractals include the popular Mandlebrot Set.

This fractal visualizer is capable of visualizing any complex equation, but the one set in this demo is a modified version of the Julia Set.

Clicking and dragging anywhere in the fractal image will generate a new zoom box allowing a closer look into the fractal.
