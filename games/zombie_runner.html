Zombie Runner

An evade-based game where collecting coins enables players to purchase power-ups to increase their zombie running performance.

An interesting game mechanic found in this game is that zombies are allowed full 360 degrees of motion while the player is constrained to only 4 directions of freedom: left, right, down, and up.

CONTROLS:
Arrows - Movement
Space Bar - Deploy Bomb (once unlocked)
C - Freeze Gun (once unlocked)
Z - Zoom Belt (once unlocked)
